The meaning of the WEDDING RING

Wedding rings are sacred, symbols of the relationship of marriage. One of the most important things to buy before getting married is the wedding ring. However, many people do not know the meaning of the wedding ring, and also do not understand why the wedding ring is worn at the thumb ring. Let Jewelry Jewelry explore this secret.

Many historians have suggested that wedding rings are of European origin, when people see the circle as a symbol of perfection, balance, and perfection. In Chinese, the wedding ring is interpreted in accordance with the moral conception of conjugal life. The word "ring" in the script is iconic knife near the heart, showing patience, patient, if hot, knife will not be afraid to hurt the heart. From here, the meaning of the wedding ring is the reminder of the new couple patiently, mutually condolences in the marriage.
